Filtering Connections

It is commonly the case that the dumpfile captured for analysis by tcptrace has much more connections than the ones you may be interested in. In such cases, it could be useful to pick and choose the connections of interest from the dumpfile, and perform detailed analysis on them alone. This chapter describes how to do such connection filtering.
